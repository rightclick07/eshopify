Delivery and Shipping Policy

At eVerse, we are committed to providing a seamless shopping experience for our customers. Below, you'll find details about our delivery and shipping policy:

Shipping Times

We strive to process and ship orders as quickly as possible. Our estimated shipping times are:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 1-3 business days

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and your location. You can view the shipping cost during checkout before making a payment.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your order on our website.

International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. Please note that international shipping may incur additional fees and longer delivery times.

Return and Refund Policy

For information about our return and refund policy, please visit our Return Policy page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our delivery and shipping policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
