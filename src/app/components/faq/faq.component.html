Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

Placing an order is simple! Just browse our website, select the drone or accessories you want to purchase, and add them to your cart. Then proceed to the checkout page, provide the required details, and complete the payment. Once the order is confirmed, we will process and ship it to you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. During the checkout process, you can choose the payment method that is most convenient for you.

What is your return policy?

We have a hassle-free return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. Please refer to our Returns & Refunds page for detailed instructions and conditions.
